History of Both Car Brands

Lamborghini started as a tractor making buisness after World War 2 and used the surplus of military machines for parts for the tractors. The creater started having problems with his Ferrari and decided he was going to create his own rival sports car company. The company started in 1963 in Italy and has been a growing company ever since.

Pagani started in 1992. The founder always loved cars at a young age and had a dream of building a supercar. The founder started working for Lamborghini and then he went out on his own after he helped them build the Countach. The founder of Pagani wanted to make light cars out of carbon fiber in order to make them faster.

Specs of The Cars In Order Below

Lamborghini Huracán: Engine- 5.2 Liter V-10. This model is AWD so it gets 631 horsepower. It can go 0 to 60 in 2.5 seconds and gets through a quarter-mile in 10.4 seconds. The transmission for this car a 7-speed dual-clutch.

Lamborghini Veneno: Engine-6.5 Liter V-12. It has a 7 speed autamated manual transmission. The car can go 0 to 60 in 2.9 seconds and creates 1.41 G while taking corners.

Lamborghini Gallardo: Engine- 5.2 Liter V-10. It has a 6-speed manual with a rear-wheel drivetrain. It also comes with a 6-speed Graziano manual or a 6-speed e-gear Graziano automated manual. Each model has a different 0 to 60 time but they are all in between 3.0 and 4.0 seconds.

Pagani Huayra L'Ultimo: Engine- 6.0 Liter V-12. This car I have pictured gets around 789 horsepower. It can go 0 to 60 in 2.8 seconds. It can come with a 7-speed automated manual transmission or a 6-speed sequential manual.

Pagani Zonda: This car has two different engine types and they are tge 6.0-7.3 Liter V-12 and a 6.9-7.3 Liter v-12. It also has 4 transmission types a 5-speed manual, a 6-speed manual, a 6-speed automated manual, and a 7-speed automated manual. IT can go 0 to 60 in 4.0 seconds.

Pagani Imola: This car has a Twin-Turbocharged SOHC V-12 6.0 Liter with a 7-speed sequential manual and real wheel drive. It goes 0 to 60 in 2.8 seconds.
